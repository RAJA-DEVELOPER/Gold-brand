Boutique FAQ

Do I need an appointment to visit?
While we welcome walk-in visitors, we highly recommend booking a private viewing to ensure a curator is available to provide undivided attention and a personalized selection of pieces.
Can I pick up online orders in-store?
Yes, our 'Boutique Collection' service allows you to purchase online and collect your piece at any of our global ateliers, accompanied by a complimentary fitting.
Do you offer valuation services?
We provide professional appraisal and valuation certificates for all Tanishq pieces. For heritage jewelry from other houses, please book a specialist consultation.
