Assurance

Common Inquiries

How accurate is the jewelry size?

Our AI uses the relative distance of your features (like your eyes or nose) to calculate your silhouette's scale, ensuring the jewelry is displayed in its true physical proportions.

Are my photos stored?

Absolutely not. For your privacy, all image processing occurs locally on your browser. Your portrait never leaves your device and is deleted as soon as you close the tab.

Can I try on custom designs?

Yes. Once you start a bespoke journey with our designers, we can upload your custom 3D model to your private 'Digital Atelier' for you to try on before crafting begins.

What lighting works best?

For the best results, we recommend natural, soft lighting from the front. Avoid harsh shadows or backlighting to allow the AI to track facial features accurately.

Uncompromising Safety

Our Security Protocol

Tanishq values your privacy as much as our diamonds. Our Virtual Try-On is built on a foundation of 'Zero-Knowledge' architecture.

Edge Computing: 100% of the image analysis happens on your local processor. We never see your face.

Auto-Purge: All temporary cache data is wiped automatically when you navigate away from the page.

No Cookies: We do not use tracking cookies for the try-on session. Your identity remains yours alone.
