Pricing FAQ

How often are the gold rates updated?
Our dashboard pulls data every 60 seconds from global bullion markets. However, the store billing rate is set once daily at 11:00 AM based on the opening market price.
What are 'Making Charges'?
Making charges cover the artisanal labor, waste (fire loss), and intricate detailing of a piece. At Tanishq, our making charges are transparently listed starting from 8% for basic designs.
Can I exchange my old gold?
Yes, we offer a Gold Exchange program where we test your old gold's purity in your presence and offer the full current market value against a new Tanishq purchase.
Are the prices inclusive of tax?
The rates shown on this dashboard are base market rates. Final invoices will include applicable GST and local luxury taxes as per government regulations.
