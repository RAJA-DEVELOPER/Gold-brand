Caring for Your Tanishq

Maintaining the Brilliance

Your Tanishq piece is designed to last generations. Proper care ensures its brilliance remains untarnished. We recommend regular professional cleanings and careful storage.

Book Professional Cleaning
Proper Storage

Store pieces separately in lined boxes to avoid scratches.

Gentle Cleaning

Use mild soap and warm water for home maintenance.

Avoid Chemicals

Remove jewelry before using harsh cleaning agents.

Regular Checks
