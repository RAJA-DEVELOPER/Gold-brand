The Beginning

Born in the Heart of Paris

Tanishq began in 1998, in a sun-drenched atelier on Rue Saint-Honor . Founder Etienne Moreau, a fourth-generation goldsmith, sought to bridge the gap between traditional Parisian craftsmanship and contemporary elegance.

Our name, French for 'Light', represents our obsession with how diamonds and metals interact with the world around them. We don't just create objects; we capture light and freeze it in time.

Message from the Founder

"Jewelry is the most intimate form of art. It carries our memories, our promises, and our legacy. At Tanishq, we honor that trust with every cut of a diamond and every pour of gold."

We remain a family-owned house, independent and focused solely on the perfection of our craft. My promise to you is a piece that will be as brilliant on your great-grandchild as it is on you today.

Etienne Moreau

The Journey

Evolution of Elegance

1998

Atelier Saint-Honor

The first Tanishq boutique opens in Paris, specializing in bespoke engagement rings for the city's elite.

2005

The Milan Annex

We expanded to Milan, integrating Italian gold-smithing techniques into our Parisian design language.

2012

Global Sourcing Integrity

Tanishq achieved full RJC certification, guaranteeing 100% ethical sourcing for all precious stones.

2023

Digital Renaissance

Launch of our worldwide digital boutique and Virtual Try-On AR technology, bringing the showroom to you.

Our Commitment

Purity Beyond the Gemstone

Conflict-Free Assurance

Every diamond in the Tanishq vault is tracked via Blockchain technology from the moment it is unearthed. We strictly adhere to the Kimberley Process, ensuring our brilliance never comes at a human cost.

Circular Gold Economy

By 2027, 100% of our gold will be sourced from recycled electronic and medical-grade archives, reducing our carbon footprint by 84% without losing a single karat of purity.

Artisanal Preservation

We invest 5% of all proceeds into traditional goldsmithing schools in Jaipur and Vicenza, ensuring that the ancient arts are passed down to the next generation of masters.

Giving Back

The Tanishq Foundation

We believe that beauty should empower those who create it. Through the Tanishq Foundation, we provide healthcare and education to mining communities in Botswana and Sri Lanka.

Since 2010, we have funded the construction of 14 primary schools and provided clean water access to over 50,000 people. Our commitment to the Tanishq brand legacy is equaled only by our commitment to the people who build it.
