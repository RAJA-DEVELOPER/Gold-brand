The Origins

Knowledge is Brilliance

Expert Insights for Collectors

1. Understanding Provenance

For the serious collector, the history of a piece is as valuable as the stones themselves. Our 'Héritage' collection preserves the provenance of every design, ensuring that you aren't just buying jewelry, but a piece of history. Always ask for certification that tracks the ethical journey of your gemstone.

2. The Investment Value of Rare Gems

While diamonds are classic, rare colored gemstones like Pigeon-Blood Rubies and Paraiba Tourmalines have shown significant appreciation in the global market. The Tanishq catalog highlights these 'investment-grade' pieces to help you build a portfolio of wearable wealth.

3. Metal Purity & Structural Integrity

We exclusively use 18K Gold and Platinum 950. While 24K gold is pure, it is too soft for intricate stone settings. 18K provides the perfect balance of rich color and the strength needed to hold multi-carat centerpieces securely for generations.

Collector's Tip

"Never store pearls with diamonds; the hardness of the diamond can easily scratch the soft nacre of the pearl. Use our climate-controlled museum boxes for optimal preservation."
